# Liouville-type Results for Infinity Elliptic Equations

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains my research conducted at the **Vietnam Institute for Advanced Study in Mathematics (VIASM)** during the Summer 2025 Research Fellowship (Aug–Nov 2025).

The project focuses on establishing **Liouville-type results** for a class of **infinity elliptic equations** involving:

* Gradient-dependent nonlinearities
* Hardy–Hénon type nonlinearities

These results contribute to the theoretical understanding of nonlinear partial differential equations and their qualitative behavior.

## 🧾 Abstract

We investigate Liouville-type properties for a class of infinity elliptic equations with nonlinearities depending on both the gradient and spatial weights of Hardy–Hénon type. By employing advanced techniques in nonlinear analysis and elliptic PDE theory, we establish nonexistence results under suitable structural conditions. These findings extend classical Liouville theorems and provide new insights into the qualitative behavior of solutions.
